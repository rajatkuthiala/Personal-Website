Projects

Self Parking Car

Parking inside a crowded garage can be difficult and though a car might fit in the space the driver may find it difficult to exit the vehicle. Using cameras, cars can be trained to be autonomous using machine learning models such as convolutional neural networks (CNN). This project is used to explore the autonomous parking into a garage using a remote controlled car as a test vehicle.

Headphones Amplifier

Buying headphone amplifiers can take a toll on your wallet, so we built a custom designed amplifier under $30 with load impedence of 30-200Ω using LT Spice and custom PCB designing. The amplifier is designed provided low noice interference and high audio quality.
